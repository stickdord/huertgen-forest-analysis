## Issue 1: Impact of Terrain
**Label:** `terrain`

**Description:** Using the AAR, identify and list specific terrain features mentioned (e.g., ridges, gorges, forest type, roads) and explain their direct impact on the regiment's tactics, mobility, and logistical support.

## Issue 2: Civilian Presence
**Label:** `civilians`

**Description:** Extract all mentions of civilians or civilian infrastructure in the report. Analyze how the evacuation and abandoned towns influenced military planning or the battlefield itself.

## Issue 3: Reconstruct the Order of Battle (OB)
**Label:** `OB`

**Description:** Identify all friendly and enemy military units mentioned in the AAR. Create a simple list detailing the unit type (e.g., regiment, battalion, division) and its role in the operation.

## Issue 4: Timeline of Key Events
**Label:** `timeline`

**Description:** Create a chronological list of the key events, movements, and decisions described in the AAR between 16-28 October 1944.
